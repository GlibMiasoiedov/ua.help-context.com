# UA Help Context

## Company name / design system name

UA Help Context

## Blurb

UA Help Context is a Ukrainian informational and promotional web project focused on giving people clear context, guidance, and practical next steps. The brand should feel trustworthy, modern, calm, human, and useful.

## Brand voice

Calm, clear, supportive, practical, direct. Use simple language, short phrases, and focus on help, trust, clarity, and action.

## Visual direction

Modern minimal interface, strong readability, clean layouts, generous spacing, high contrast, Ukrainian blue and yellow accents, simple icons, human-centered visuals, and practical promotional compositions.

## Avoid

Avoid war cliches, excessive flags, chaotic layouts, overly dramatic dark visuals, tiny text, generic corporate stock imagery, and aggressive sales language.
